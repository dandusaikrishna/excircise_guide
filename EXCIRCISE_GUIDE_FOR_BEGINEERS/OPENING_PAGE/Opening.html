Diet Plan

Follow a balanced and nutritious diet plan tailored to complement your fitness goals. Here’s a sample:

  • Morning
    • Eggs – A great source of protein to kickstart your day.
    • Milk – Rich in calcium and essential nutrients for strong bones.
    • Bread with Peanut Butter – A perfect mix of carbs and healthy fats.
  • Afternoon
    • Grilled Chicken or Tofu – High in protein and low in fat.
    • Brown Rice – A healthy carb option to fuel your afternoon.
    • Steamed Vegetables – Packed with vitamins and fiber.
  • Evening Snack
    • Fruits – A natural source of energy and antioxidants.
    • Greek Yogurt – High in protein and probiotics for digestion.
    • Nuts and Seeds – Healthy fats and nutrients in every bite.
  • Night
    • Grilled Fish or Lentil Soup – Light yet protein-rich options for dinner.
    • Quinoa or Sweet Potato – Complex carbs for sustained energy.
    • Fresh Salad – Keeps your meal refreshing and nutritious.

Remember, consistency in both diet and exercise is key to a healthier you. Consult a dietician or nutritionist for personalized meal plans tailored to your fitness goals.
